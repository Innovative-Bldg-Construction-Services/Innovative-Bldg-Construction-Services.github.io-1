Introduction

Challenges

Waterproofing is one of the most critical, yet neglected subject because of various reasons associated with concrete technology and selection of product/system specification. Most of the time waterproofing is considered as inconvenience rather than a serious matter. Water infiltration causes major problems to a structure. Water damages a building first cosmetically then structurally. It is important to realize that by the time a stain shows up on the interior of your building most likely irreversible damage has been caused to the structure.

  • Corrosion of metals such as steel reinforcement in concrete, steel beams, lintels, etc causing malignancy.
  • Possible short circuit of lighting and power points because of electrical hazards
  • Blistering of Paint
  • Damage to structures and finishes such as spalling paint, cement plaster, cover concrete in ceiling, beams, columns.
  • Health problems due to dampness, which may lead to respiratory problems, growth of microorganisms and exposure to gas like radon.

Solutions

This is a very complicated problem with no single solution. Hence, we at IBCS evaluate each given building conditions and recommend the most suitable product and systems from various manufacturers. We at ICBS are experienced in executing the following areas to make your building completely watertight:

  • Basement floor, basement retaining walls
  • Lift pit, sump tank areas
  • Wet areas like, toilet, bath, balcony
  • Podium landscaping and hardscape areas
  • Terrace areas
  • Overhead water tank
  • STP, ETP tanks
  • Swimming pools and waterbodies
  • External façade
  • Expansion joint
