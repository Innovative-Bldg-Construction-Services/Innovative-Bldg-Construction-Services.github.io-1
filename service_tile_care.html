Introduction

Challenges

The surface to be tiled (floor, wall or any other) will often need to be prepared. This can include work like filling, levelling, smoothing, priming and waterproofing in wet areas.

The right preparation will directly affect the lifespan of the tile installation. Unless one wants to be tiling again soon, one wants to get this right from the start. It is important to follow all the necessary steps.

Solutions

Here at IBCS we are specialized in preparatory works, waterproofing, screeding and levelling which will help achieving a successful and long-lasting tile installation. We also use polymerbased tile adhesives to ensure that there are no debonding due to shrinkage of cement and weathering actions.

Furthermore, we have the expertise about tile and stone care, which means, the final handing over of the tiled surface is always clean free from dust, dirt, stains, or any other contaminants as we use the best in class tile care cleaners from the range of heavy duty and non-acidic cleaners.

Our Undertakings

For long lasting maintenance of natural and artificial stones, we do have wide range of sealers like Silicate, Silane, Flouro-carbon, water based, solvent based, Hydrophobic and Oleophobic. We deal in following:

  • Under-tile sealing
  • Tile and stone installation
  • Tile and stone sealers
  • Stone restoration and maintenance
