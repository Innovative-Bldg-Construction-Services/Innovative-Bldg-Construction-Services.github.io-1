Introduction

Why Resin Floor

Industrial resin flooring can be applied to offer protection to the concrete floor surface against everyday wear and tear and chemical spillage. It can be applied in a non-slip finish for safety requirements, as a performance flooring to resist chemicals, impact, temperature (Extreme cold and Hot), as a decorative finish to enhance the appearance in the workplace. From the application of a basic sealer coat, through to a thicker trowel applied resin floor screed, IBCS have many years practical experience in the application of industrial resin floor systems.

Types

There are different types of resin flooring systems available in Indian market that are normally divided into epoxy, polyurethane and PMMA (Polymethyl methacrylate resins). The different types of resin provide different combinations of application characteristics and through life performance and there are a number of factors to consider when selecting a resin flooring system. At IBCS we have the knowledge and expertise to offer you sound professional advice on the most appropriate and cost-effective resin flooring system to meet your operational requirements as per BS 8204-6.

Our Approach

Critical to the success of any resin flooring system application is the degree of surface preparation carried out before the resin can be laid. It is part of our philosophy that detailed surface preparation is carried out on all our projects, where we will pay particular attention to:

  • Floor Joints – Location, Type and Condition
  • Surface Contamination – Laitance, Oils, grease, etc
  • Cracking to the surface
  • Service Conditions (how the floor is expected to be used)
